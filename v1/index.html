A Message from Our Director
As Director of the National Institute of Technology, I’m thrilled to welcome you. Here, we blend rigorous academics with practical innovation, preparing students to tackle global challenges. Our dedicated faculty and advanced facilities support our mission to foster leaders in technology and engineering.

I encourage you to explore our site and see how you can join us in making a difference. Our alumni, spread across the globe, are a testament to our educational ethos, contributing significantly to the technological advancement and societal development. As we stride forward, we remain committed to fostering an inclusive, diverse, and equitable environment where every student can achieve their full potential.
Warm regards,
Dr. N V Ramana Rao
